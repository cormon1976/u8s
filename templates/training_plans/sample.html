Excercise 1 Objectives

Striking the ball at the correct height between the knee and Hip. Following the key concepts above

  • Total Time. 7
  • Hold the ball on a stick at knee height and ask the players to strike it hard. No foot movement allowed, follow through on the strike, hit the ball between players knee and hip height.

Excercise 2 Objectives

2a: Throw the ball up and catch it 15 times.
2b: Throw it up and strike it with one hand against the fence in the style of a tennis forehand 10 times
Repeat those two steps 3 times.

  • 2a: Each player standing at the cones facing out to the mentors.
  • Throw it up and catch it, eye height and keeping eyes on the ball at all time.
  • 2b: Each player standing at the cones facing towards the outside net.
